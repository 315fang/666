# Logo Design Prompt: Spring Festival (CNY) Edition

## Context
This logo is for the Lunar New Year (Spring Festival) celebration. It should evoke warmth, celebration, and prosperity while maintaining brand recognition.

## Requirements
- **Format**: SVG / High-res PNG
- **Background**: Transparent
- **Style**: Festive, Warm, Traditional elements with modern twist
- **Colors**: Primary Red (#E60012) and Gold (#FFD700).
- **Shape**: Rounded, enclosed (like a coin or lantern).

## AI Prompts (Copy & Paste to Figma AI / Midjourney / DALL-E)

### Option 1: Lantern Concept
> A minimalist logo design inspired by a Chinese lantern. Clean vector lines. Geometric. Central element glowing. Symbolizing light and celebration. Red and Gold colors. Flat design, app icon style.

### Option 2: Chinese Knot / Fortune
> A stylized Chinese knot design forming a modern geometric logo. Symmetrical, balanced. Represents unity and prosperity. Solid red lines on white. Vector graphic.

### Option 3: Paper Cut Style
> A modern logo with a paper-cut art texture. Festive floral patterns integrated into a simple circular shape. Negative space design. Red background with white cutouts. Cultural and artistic.

## Usage Note
Ensure the red is vibrant but not eye-straining. Gold should look like a flat yellow-orange gradient for better digital visibility. Keep details minimal to avoid clutter at small sizes.
