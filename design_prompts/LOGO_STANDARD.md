# Logo Design Prompt: Standard Professional Edition

## Context
This logo is for a S2B2C (Supply Chain to Business to Consumer) e-commerce platform. The design needs to be professional, modern, and trustworthy.

## Requirements
- **Format**: SVG / High-res PNG
- **Background**: Transparent
- **Style**: Minimalist, Modern, Tech-focused
- **Colors**: Trustworthy Blue (#007AFF) or Energetic Orange (#FF5722) combined with deep grey/black.
- **Shape**: Simple geometric shapes, suitable for small icon usage (WeChat Mini Program).

## AI Prompts (Copy & Paste to Figma AI / Midjourney / DALL-E)

### Option 1: Abstract Connection (Focus on S2B2C)
> A modern, minimalist logo for an e-commerce platform. Abstract geometric shapes representing connection and supply chain. Three interlinked elements forming a unified structure. Professional corporate style. Solid colors, flat design, vector style. Primary color: Deep Blue. White background. High contrast.

### Option 2: Letter Mark (Monogram)
> A stylized letter mark logo for a brand. Modern typography-based design. Bold, clean lines. Intertwined lines suggesting flow and commerce. Minimalist vector art. Tech startup aesthetic. 

### Option 3: Abstract Box/Cube (Focus on Logistics/Product)
> A minimalist isometric cube logo. Represents a package or digital product. Clean lines, open corners. Gradient blue to cyan. Modern app icon style. Simple, memorable, scalable.

## Usage Note
When using Figma AI, select "Logo" or "Icon" style and set the complexity to "Low" or "Medium" for best results on small screens.
