# Logo Design Prompt: Horse Year Edition (2026)

## Context
This logo is a special edition for the Year of the Horse (2026). It should retain the brand's professional feel but incorporate Horse elements to symbolize energy, speed, and success.

## Requirements
- **Format**: SVG / High-res PNG
- **Background**: Transparent
- **Style**: Dynamic, Energetic, Cultural but Modern
- **Colors**: Red, Gold, or Dynamic gradients.
- **Shape**: Fluid lines, silhouette.

## AI Prompts (Copy & Paste to Figma AI / Midjourney / DALL-E)

### Option 1: Minimalist Horse Silhouette
> A minimalist logo of a horse head in profile. Single continuous line art. Elegant and dynamic. Modern vector style. Symbolizing speed and success. Color: Red and Gold. Flat design, white background.

### Option 2: Abstract Motion
> Abstract geometric shapes forming a running horse. Forward momentum. Tech-inspired polygon art style. Low poly design. Sharp angles. Energetic orange and yellow gradients. Modern e-commerce branding.

### Option 3: Calligraphic Fusion
> A fusion of a modern logo icon with a stylized brush stroke resembling a horse. East meets West design. Ink wash painting style but vectorized and clean. Red stamp aesthetic.

## Usage Note
Focus on the *silhouette* and *motion* of the horse. Avoid overly detailed realistic illustrations. The logo needs to remain legible when shrunk to the size of a WeChat Mini Program icon.
